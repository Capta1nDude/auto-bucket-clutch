<h2>About</h2>

<p>
    Automatically uses a water bucket to clutch when falling! Comes with a bunch of extra configs.
</p>

<h2>Features</h2>

<ul>
  <li>Toggle automatic water bucket placing when you're falling to stop taking fall damage</li>
  <li>Hotkey to quickly switch a bucket into your hand</li>
  <li>Configure activation height, auto pickup, preffered slots and more with Mod Menu!</li>
</ul>

<h2>FAQ</h2>

**Q: How do I turn on and off?**  
A: Default on/off hotkey is G. Look in the vanilla hotkeys menu to change

**Q: How do I configure?**
A: Using Mod Menu, in the mod menu hover over the Auto Bucket Clutch icon and press the configure icon, which will open the configuration menu.
